THE BEST 3 MOVIES ACCORDING TO MANPREET

My top 3 Movies of all time

Chup Chupke

A funny take on the life of a messed up commoner who gets a second go at life in the most bizarre and humourous way.

Bhool Bhulaiya

If you are a fan of horror movies but want to have a good time as well, you are in for a ride in this mythical masterpiece starring phenomenal Vidya Balan, Akshay Kumar, Paresh Rawal and other great actors/actresses.

Badla

With the starcast of laden names such as Amitabh Bachchan, Tapsee Pannu, Amrita Singh; this movie captures the essence of true mystery thriller by showcasing human's manipulating tendencies and notions of grief and forgiveness. This movie will leave you with a chilling experience and a strong message.
